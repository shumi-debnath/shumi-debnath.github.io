Lights Up

Lights Up is a san serif typeface that can be used for headings or used as a decorative font. When making this typeface, I wanted to start off with a black box and use different shapes to carve out from the box to create that letter. I also tried to make it asymmertrical and I had it so that the shapes would be on the right half of the box. For the R, I used a semi circle and a right triangle to cut out the letter. For the A, I used a right triangle and a square. For the N, I used two right triangles. For the G, I used three rectangles. For the E, I used two rectangles. I did the same thing for the S.
