Egg Fried Rice w/ Veggies

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Scallions
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Cut vegetables into small, thin slices
  3. Cut scallions diagonally and thinly
  4. Sautee vegetables in pot over high heat
  5. Pour rice into the pot and mix
  6. Plate the egg fried rice with vegetables and add soy sauce.
